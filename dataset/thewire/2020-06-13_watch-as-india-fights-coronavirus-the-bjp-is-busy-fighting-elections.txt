Watch | 'As India Fights Coronavirus, the BJP is Busy Fighting Elections'
What does the situation in Rajasthan say?

On the one hand, the global pandemic has raged across India and the numbers grow worryingly in places. On the other hand, politics has been progressing as usual. In Rajasthan, ahead of the Rajya Sabha election, the Congress and its supporting legislators reached the Chief Minister’s residence on Wednesday evening. Senior editor Arfa Khanum Sherwani speaks on the BJP’s role in the political turn to political affairs editor Ajoy Ashirwad, and journalist Avinash Kalla.
