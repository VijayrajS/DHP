Watch | Does Self-Reliance Mean Each of Us Must Deal With COVID-19 By Ourselves?
With malls, religious places and hotels reopening, Arfa Khanum Sherwani discusses what impact this decision may have on the coronavirus cases in India. 

From Monday, religious places, malls, restaurants and hotels were opened in most parts of the country after nearly 70 days of being shut down. Although the government has asked everyone to follow preventive measures such as social distancing, India is reporting one of the highest new cases in the world everyday. Is this the right time to open the lockdown, will the ‘Unlock 1’ not increase the number of people who have COVID-19? Arfa Khanum Sherwani discusses these issues with professor Vikas Bajpai and economist Jayati Ghosh.
