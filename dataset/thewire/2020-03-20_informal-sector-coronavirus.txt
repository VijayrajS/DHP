Watch | How India's Informal Sector Stopped Dead as a Result of the Coronavirus Pandemic
How have daily labourers and small shopkeepers fared?

Schools, colleges and other crowded places have been closed due to the increase in the cases of COVID-19. In such a situation, private companies are also advising employees to work from home.
But this has had a serious impact on the unorganised sector of the country. Daily labourers and small shopkeepers have been facing a clear struggle, as documented by The Wire’s Srishti Srivastava.
