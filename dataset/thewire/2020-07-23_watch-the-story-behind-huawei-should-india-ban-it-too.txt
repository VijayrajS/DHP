Watch | The Story Behind Huawei: Should India Ban It Too?
Mitali Mukherjee explains what India's approach should be to the tech giant in the backdrop of the country's border conflict with China.

The accusations that China did not take enough steps to control the coronavirus from spreading have led to a strong anti-Chinese trade wave across the world. One company has been singled out for a backlash – Huawei, a tech company with its fingers in diverse pies, from telecom infrastructure to handsets to chipsets. But what’s the Huawei story and why are countries stonewalling this tech giant? More importantly, what should India’s approach to the tech entity be in the backdrop of the country’s border conflict with China?
