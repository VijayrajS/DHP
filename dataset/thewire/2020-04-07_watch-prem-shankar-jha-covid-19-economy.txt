Watch | 'The Harm to Economy Can Still Be Contained if Govt Acts Now': Prem Shankar Jha
Prem Shankar Jha speaks to The Wire on the single biggest threat to independent India.

Eighty-one-year-old veteran journalist Prem Shankar Jha has been a columnist and editor of various newspapers including the Hindustan Times for 55 years. Jha speaks to The Wire on the single most threatening crisis India has faced since the country was born, the coronavirus pandemic.
