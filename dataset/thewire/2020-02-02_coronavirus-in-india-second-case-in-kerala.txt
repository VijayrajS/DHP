Coronavirus in India: Second Case Reported in Kerala
The first novel coronavirus case was also reported from Kerala.
Hospital staff is seen outside the Special Isolation Ward set up to provide treatment to any suspected case of the coronavirus (CoV) at the Rajiv Gandhi Government General Hospital, in Chennai, Tuesday, Jan. 28, 2020. Photo: PTI 
New Delhi: India on Sunday reported the second case of novel coronavirus with a person from Kerala with a travel history to China testing positive, officials said.
“The patient has tested positive for novel coronavirus and is in isolation in a hospital,” the health ministry said.
