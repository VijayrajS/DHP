Watch | How Are Cab and Auto Drivers Coping With COVID-19 and the Lockdown?
After the lifting of the lockdown, cab and auto drivers not been able to make ends meet.

As a result of the coronavirus pandemic and the subsequent lockdown, many businesses have suffered losses and some have ground to a complete halt. A large number of people have lost their jobs and many workers have not been paid their dues.
In India, the lockdown has now been lifted and people have started returning to the ‘normal’. But after the lifting of the lockdown, why have cab and auto drivers not been able to make ends meet?
