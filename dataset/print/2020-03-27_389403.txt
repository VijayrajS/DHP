Corona Warriors or Covid Warriors are the two terms that have become synonymous with India‚Äôs healthcare workers and others involved in the fight against coronavirus. The fact is that India is waging a war against an unseen enemy. And this is no ordinary battle.

While I have argued in the past¬†against random deployment of military for law and order breakdowns, the current situation warrants that the defence forces be deployed against coronavirus.
