Tribute to Sushant Singh Rajput and PM Oli’s cartography lessons
The selected cartoons appeared first in other publications, either in print or online, or on social media, and are credited appropriately.
In today’s featured cartoon, Mir Suhail pays tribute to actor Sushant Singh Rajput who was found dead at his residence in Mumbai Sunday.
Sajith Kumar takes a jibe at Nepal’s new controversial map, which shows three places that are disputed between New Delhi and Kathmandu as being within the Nepalese borders, and hints that China could be behind the move.
Sandeep Adhwaryu pokes fun at incidents of ‘data fudging’. The Delhi government was recently questioned for allegedly manipulating Covid-19 data.
