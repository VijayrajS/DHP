Delhi police arrests two for operating gym despite closure order in wake of coronavirus
New Delhi: Two gym owners were arrested by the Delhi Police for violating the Delhi government directive on immediate shut down of gymnasiums, spas and other such facilities till 31 March in the wake of coronavirus pandemic.
Four FIRs were registered by the west district police against gyms in the area for violating the government order. “While two gym owners have been arrested, action is being initiated against the rest,” Deepak Purohit (IPS), DCP, West District told ANI. FIRs were registered under Sections 188 and 269 of the Indian Penal Code and Section 3 Epidemic Diseases Act 1897.
