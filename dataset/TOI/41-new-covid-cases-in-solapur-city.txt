41 new Covid cases in Solapur city
2020-06-14
Kolhapur: Solapur city has reported 41 new Covid-19 cases and six more deaths on Saturday. Six patients were discharged on Saturday. The total number of cases in the city is 1544, with 802 patients discharged and 133 casualties
