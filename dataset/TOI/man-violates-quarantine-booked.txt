Man violates quarantine, booked
2020-03-30
Vadodara: An offence was registered against a man from Dahod for breaking home quarantine directives. The man had returned from Dubai and was asked to stay in isolation at home for two weeks.

Kamlesh Bamaniya, a resident of Kharoda village of Dahod district, had returned from Dubai on March 19. Initially, he came to Vadodara and then went to Kharoda. He was advised home quarantine and health teams used to keep a tab on his health. On Friday Bamaniya had left home and had gone to different villages in Garbada taluka. On Saturday too, he had gone to Rentiya village where he was confronted by health workers. An offence was registered against him and he has been sent to a quarantine facility in Dahod.
