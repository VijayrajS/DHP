Home quarantine now 7 days for all arriving in Delhi
2020-06-04
The 14-day mandatory home quarantine for all asymptomatic passengers arriving in Delhi was on Wednesday reduced to seven days by the state government. 

The order was issued according to the guidelines of the ministry of health and family welfare.

Chief secretary Vijay Dev directed airport, railway and transport department authorities to submit passenger lists to the revenue department daily.
