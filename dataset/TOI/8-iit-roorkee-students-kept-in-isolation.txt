Coronavirus in Uttarakhand: 8 IIT-Roorkee students kept in isolation
2020-03-16
ROORKEE: IIT-Roorkee has reportedly kept eight students, including one of foreign origin, in isolation at a guest house in the institute as a preventive measure. 



Coronavirus outbreak live updates


Sources told TOI that all the students, who are being kept in isolation, had recently returned from abroad. 

An institute official, who did not wish to be named, said, “These students are under watch for the past 14 days and will be discharged after they complete the incubation period.”

Meanwhile, the institute has claimed that its campus is safe from the outbreak of the virus. 

“There is no Covid-19 positive case on the campus,” said director AK Chaturvedi.
