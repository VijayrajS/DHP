No relaxation in lockdown from today
2020-04-20
Varanasi: The district will not get any relaxation in lockdown norms from Monday as Covid-19 lockdown will remain enforced strictly like past 27 days.

District magistrate Kaushal Raj Sharma said that as the state government has ordered no change in arrangements prescribed during lockdown in the districts where number of positive cases is more than 10, no further activity or services will be allowed in Varanasi.

Shops of essential commodities, including grocery, milk, vegetable, fruit and others will be closed at 10 am now instead of 11 am after getting opened at 6 am, said the DM. 

The wholesale markets of medicine and other goods will be opened like past days of lockdown period. Maintaining social distance will be a must, he said.
