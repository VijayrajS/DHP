Pune: Katraj zoo initiates steps to avoid spread of coronavirus to wildlife
2020-04-13
PUNE: The management of Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park, or Katraj zoo, has started taking preventive measures to curb any possibility of the spread of coronavirus from human to animals. 


Use of PPE kits has been made mandatory for zoo staff. Personal hygiene norms and sanitization has been implemented strictly. The animals will have to undergo medical tests only if they are found to be symptomatic.
