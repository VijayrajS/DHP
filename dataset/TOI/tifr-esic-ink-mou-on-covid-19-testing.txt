TIFR, ESIC ink MoU on Covid-19 testing
2020-04-30
HYDERABAD: Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Hyderabad and Employees State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) Hospital and Medical College, Hyderabad, signed a MoU for developing tests that can rapidly confirm Covid-19 infections. 

ESIC hospital will be testing the assays on human samples for purposes of validation. TIFR has contributed to the infrastructure by providing RT-PCR machine of ESIC Covid-19 testing facility. “TIFR Hyderabad will provide RNA prep and RT-PCR training to contribute towards generating a skilled workforce,” said TIFR.
