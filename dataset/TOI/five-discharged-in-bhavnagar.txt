Five Covid-19 patients discharged after recovery in Bhavnagar
2020-05-17
RAJKOT: Five persons, including three from the same family, were discharged from Sir T hospital of Bhavnagar after they recovered from Covid-19 infection, on Saturday.

According to doctors, Ranjitsinh Machchhar (27), Agam Vora (18), Anand Vora (18), Angi Vora (13) and Jubeda Pathan (38) were discharged on Saturday. All these patients are discharged as per new guidelines after completion of 10-day hospitalisation.
