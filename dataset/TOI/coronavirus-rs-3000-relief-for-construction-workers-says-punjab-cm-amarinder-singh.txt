Coronavirus: Rs 3,000 relief for construction workers, says Punjab CM Amarinder Singh
2020-03-22
CHANDIGARH: Amid the Covid-19 restrictions, Punjab CM Amarinder Singh on Saturday announced immediate relief of Rs 3,000 each for all registered construction workers in state. 



Coronavirus outbreak live updates


The amount would be transferred to the bank accounts of the workers by Monday in a bid to mitigate the hardships being faced by them in the wake of the outbreak, which has brought all construction activity in the state to a standstill. 

A total of Rs 96 crore will be released for this purpose, said an official spokesperson, adding that the labour department has been directed by the CM to take immediate steps to ensure the transfer by Monday.
