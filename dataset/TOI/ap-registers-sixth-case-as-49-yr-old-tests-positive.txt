Coronavirus: Andhra Pradesh registers sixth case as 49-year-old tests positive
2020-03-23
VIJAYAWADA: Andhra Pradesh registered its sixth case of Covid-19 as a 49-year-old woman from Visakhapatnam, relative of the 65-year-old Covid-19 patient, tested positive on Sunday. 


On the other hand, the first Covid-19 patient in the state from Nellore was tested negative on Sunday. The 65-year-old man from Alipuram in Vizag city had travelled to Saudi Arabia in March. He returned to the city in a train after arriving in Hyderabad and staying at a relative’s home for a day. He subsequently approached a private hospital and private diagnostics centre in Visakhapatnam after developing some symptoms. 

He was finally referred to Government Hospital for Chest and Communicable Diseases in Vizag city. He was tested positive for Covid-19 on March 19. The Vizag district authorities had carried out door-to-door survey in nearly 26,000 houses in the vicinity of the patient’s home.
