Covid-19: One under radar in Mangaluru
2020-03-09
MANGALURU: One person, who arrived at Mangalore International airport from Dubai was sent to a quarantine facility set up at District Wenlock Hospital on Sunday over suspected coronavirus infection.


District health and family welfare officer Sikandar Pasha said the passenger who arrived from Dubai showed signs of fever when screened. “As a precautionary measure, the passenger will be kept in quarantine facility overnight and discharged after a thorough check up if the person is found tests negative,” he said. 

VV Rao, airport director, MIA said all passengers arriving at the international terminal are being screened as per DGCA and ministry of civil aviation guidelines.
