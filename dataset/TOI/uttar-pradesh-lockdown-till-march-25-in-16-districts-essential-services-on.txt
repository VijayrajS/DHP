2020-03-23
LUCKNOW: In view of overwhelming response to ‘janata curfew’ observed on Sunday on the call of Prime Minister Narendra Modi, chief minister Yogi Adityanath announced a complete lockdown of 16 districts till March 25.


These districts include Lucknow, Gautam Buddh Nagar, Ghaziabad, Agra, Varanasi, Lakhimpur Kheri, Kanpur, Aligarh, Gorakhpur, Azamgarh, Saharanpur, Bareilly, Prayagraj, Meerut, Pilibhit and Moradabad.

 
 
“The lockdown has been announced to sanitise cities to stop the spread of Covid-19,” said Yogi, who himself joined people in clapping, clanging plates and blowing conch shells at 5pm to express gratitude to people at the forefront of fight against coronavirus.

Deputy chief minister Keshav Prasad Maurya also joined the people by blowing conch shell outside his official residence at Kalidas Marg.

“There is no need to panic. The decision has been taken to combat the disease at this stage in which virus spreads fast. People affected in these districts will be isolated and quarantined,” the CM said.

Yogi said the districts bordering Nepal should also take precautions and urged people, who have travelled to the Himalayan state recently, to stay indoors. “During lockdown, the state government will completely stop mass transport services including Metro trains and city buses. Intercity buses will also stop operations. Only essential services will be available,” the CM said.
