Man tests negative for coronavirus in Goa
2020-02-01
PANAJI: The quarantined patient suspected to be infected by coronavirus, was discharged from Goa Medical College (GMC)l on Friday after he tested negative.

“The person who had travelled to China was admitted to GMC on January 28 for suspected coronavirus as a preventive measure. He tested negative for the virus on Friday. After his discharge, his condition will be monitored for 28 days as per the WHO guidelines,” an official said.

Two more persons were brought under observation on Friday as they had visited China. This, is in addition to the six persons whose health is being monitored after they returned from China.

As part of the all-India surveillance measures, Goa International Airport too started screening passengers since Thursday night. The state government had also asked for scanners to be installed at the Dabolim airport.
