1 Covid patient admitted to Kottayam MCH
2020-05-21
Kottayam: A Thiruvananthapuram native confirmed with Covid-19 on Wednesday was admitted to the Government Medical College Hospital. 

The 40-year-old from Parassala, residing in Mumbai, was returning home by road from Mumbai when he was admitted in the general hospital with fever and other health issues on Monday. He was shifted to MCH on Tuesday after he experienced breathing difficulties. The sample test result of his driver is awaited.

Meanwhile, the quarantine period of first batch of 20 NRIs in the district, who arrived at Nedumbassery airport from Abu Dhabi and Dubai on May 7, is to get over on Thursday. Among them, one person was confirmed with Covid. tnn
