Lekhpal suspended, booked for refusing to join duty in lockdown
2020-03-27
Rampur: A Milak thesil lekhpal has been suspended and booked after he allegedly refused to join his duty during the covid-19 pandemic, which has been declared as a disaster by the UP government.

Following a complaint lodged by tehsildar Bimal Kumar Shukl, Milak police registered an FIR against Sumit Saxena under relevant sections of the Disaster Management Act. 

Meanwhile, Rampur district magistrate Aunjaneya Kumar Singh warned, “Stringent action will be taken against those government officials who refused to work during the lockdown.”
