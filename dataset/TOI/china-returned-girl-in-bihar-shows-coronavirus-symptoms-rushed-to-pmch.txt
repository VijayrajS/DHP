China-returned Bihar girl shows coronavirus symptoms, rushed to PMCH
2020-01-27
PATNA: A girl from Bihar was admitted to ICU of a local hospital in Chapra after she showed symptoms similar to that of coronavirus. She is now being taken to Patna Medical College and Hospital (PMCH). 

The girl had recentally returned from China.

"The girl will be shifted to Patna's PMCH," said PMCH superintendent.
