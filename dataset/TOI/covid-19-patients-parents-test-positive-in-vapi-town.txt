Gujarat: Covid-19 patient's parents test positive in Vapi town
2020-05-25
SURAT: Parents of a 31-year-old Covid-19 positive patient tested positive for the virus in Vapi town of Valsad district on Sunday. District health officials said a 55-year-old man and his 54-year-old wife, residents of Satadhar Society in Vapi town, tested positive for coronavirus.
