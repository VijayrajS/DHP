Covid-19: E-papers at Kalyan
2020-03-17
Arya Gurukul School in Kalyan conducted its last exam papers for classes V to VIII online on Monday, as schools were shut. Bharat Malik, director, Arya Global Group, said, “On Monday, we conducted a pilot run of online exams.” Principal Radhamani Iyer said, “We have planned online exams in future too to help students go digital and also help save paper and environment.”
