Union minister Prakash Javadekar sanctions Rs  1 crore to fight coronavirus in Pune
2020-03-30
PUNE: Union minister for environment, information and broadcasting Prakash Javadekar on Sunday sanctioned Rs 1 crore to deal with the Coronavirus outbreak in Pune. 

He said the funds will be given from the Member of Parliament Local Area Development (MPLAD) Fund and utilised for augmenting the infrastructure of hospitals in Pune. 

“The funds are sanctioned for procurement of ventilators, monitors, ECG machines, defibrillator, suction machines, portable X-ray machine and fogger. It will cater to the needs of the hospitals,” said Javadekar.
