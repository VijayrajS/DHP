Raj govt lax in curbing Covid spread: BJP to central panel
2020-04-26
Jaipur: State BJP president Satish Poonia on Saturday complained to the central team about laxity by the state government in containing the spread of coronavirus. A group of BJP leaders met the central team members at Jaipur collectorate. The team took stock of the health system in the state.The leaders demanded that Ramganj area should be sealed in order to contain the situation.The area has emerged as one of the biggest hotspots in the country with 500 positive cases. Meanwhile, leader of Opposition Gulab Chand Kataria slammed Congress leader Rahul Gandhi for calling reduction in the salary of Central government employees by Prime Minister Narendra Modi as inhuman.
