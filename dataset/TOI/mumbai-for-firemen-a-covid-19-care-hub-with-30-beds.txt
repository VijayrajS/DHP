Mumbai: For firemen, a Covid-19 care hub with 30 beds
2020-05-27
MUMBAI: With more than 28 fire brigade staff testing positive for Covid-19 and one fireman, a suspected case, having died, the BMC on Tuesday set up a Covid care centre in one of the buildings within the Byculla headquaters and command centre premises where it plans to treat patients with mild symptomatic or those who are asymptomatic.

Chief fire officer PS Rahangdale said, “These 30 beds will cater only to our staff who get infected while on duty. If required, we will also set up another 20-bed facility.”
