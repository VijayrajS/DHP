Capt shares Milkha’s Covid message
2020-06-11
Chandigarh: Punjab chief minister Amarinder Singh on Wednesday shared a video of Olympian Milkha Singh on Twitter, asking people to fight against coronavirus as part of the state government’s ‘Mission Fateh’ campaign. “Sharing an appeal by our ‘Flying Sikh’ Milkha Singh ji. Thank you for your time and support. I am sure all Punjabis will respond to your appeal and take necessary precautions against Covid-19 in our Mission Fateh,” said Amarinder.

In his video message, Milkha appealed to the people to wear face mask and wash their hands at least three-four times daily. “We should follow the appeal of the government. We should also maintain social distancing,” he said. tnn
