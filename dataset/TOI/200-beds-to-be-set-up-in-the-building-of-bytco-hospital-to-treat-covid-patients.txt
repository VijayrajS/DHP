Nashik: 200 beds to be set up in the building of Bytco hospital to treat Covid-19 patients
2020-05-29
NASHIK: The health department of Nashik Municipal Corporation will set up 200 beds at the new building of Bytco Hospital within 15 days to treat Covid-19 patients. 


This decision taken on Thursday comes after over 100 people had tested positive for Covid-19 in the last eight days. “This new 200-bed facility will be ready within 15 days. We have just completed electrification and lift work of the five-storied hospital building,” said NMC officials.
