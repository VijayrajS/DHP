Bengaluru: Two more suspected novel coronavirus cases in hospital
2020-01-29
BENGALURU: Two more people suspected of exposure to novel Coronavirus (2019-nCoV) were admitted to Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Chest Diseases (RGICD) on Tuesday.


Both had travelled to China last week and arrived at RGICD, complaining of symptoms of the virus.

In all, four suspected cases are currently under observation at RGICD. Another patient, whose test results came negative, has been discharged. Samples of the patients have been sent to National Institute of Virology, Pune for testing.

On Tuesday, the state health department conducted an awareness programme on coronavirus for private and government hospital doctors.
