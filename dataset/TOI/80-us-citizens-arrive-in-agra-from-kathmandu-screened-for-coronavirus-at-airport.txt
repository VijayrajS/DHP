80 US citizens arrive in Agra from Kathmandu screened for coronavirus at airport
2020-03-13
Agra: A group of 80 US citizens, who arrived from Kathmandu to Agra in a chartered plane on Thursday were screened for symptoms of the novel coronavirus (Covid-19) at the airport by a team of doctors.After visiting Australia, Combodia and Nepal, the group arrived at the Agra airport.Agra airport director Kusum Das said, “After landing at the airport, the group of 80 US nationals underwent thermal screening carried out by a team of doctors. None of the passengers had symptoms of cough, fever or cold.”Confirming the same, chief medical officer Mukesh Vats said, “The American tourists are healthy.”The US tourists are expected to stay in Agra for the next two days.
