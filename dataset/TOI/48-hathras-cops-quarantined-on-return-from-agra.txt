48 Hathras cops quarantined on return from Agra　
2020-05-06
AGRA: At least 48 police personnel from Hathras, who returned from Agra after completing their duty, were quarantined on Tuesday for 14 days as a precautionary measure.　


Confirming this, superintendent of police, Hathras, Gaurav Banswal, said, "Their samples will be taken for testing. The quarantine procedure will be followed with everyone who returns to Hathras from outside."

He said he had visited the quarantine centre earlier to check the facilities.
