Bengaluru: Padarayanapura corporator tests positive for Covid-19
2020-05-30
BENGALURU: Imran Pasha, BBMP corporator representing Padarayanpura, tested positive for coronavirus on Friday evening. 

The news spread fear among BBMP officials, who met the JD(S) corporator during the week on various occasions with regard to implementing surveillance measures in the ward. 

Padarayanapura has reported 65 positive cases till date and is still a containment zone.

Contact tracing is under way to find out his primary and secondary contacts.
