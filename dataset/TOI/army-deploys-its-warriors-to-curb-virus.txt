Secunderabad: Army deploys its ‘warriors’ to curb virus
2020-04-06
SECUNDERABAD: To curb the spread of Covid-19, the Southern Command has created several quarantine facilities. 


“The Army’s Southern Command has created several quarantine and isolation facilities in the states of Telangana, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu, and provided dedicated medical and administrative staff to take care of the patients and quarantined persons,” said a release on Friday.
