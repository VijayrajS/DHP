500 beds of MGM hospital for Covid-19
2020-05-16
AURANGABAD: Civic chief and city administrator Astik Kumar Pandey on Friday said that as many as 500 beds of Mahatma Gandhi Mission (MGM) hospital has been dedicated for treatment of Covid-19 patients.


“The hospital will be provided with personal protection equipment (PPE) kits and face masks by the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation (AMC),” he said, adding that there is no shortage of beds for treatment of Covid-19 cases.
