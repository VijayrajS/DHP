12 oppose quarantine unit, MBMC files case
2020-03-24
The Mira-Bhayander Municipal Corporation has filed police cases against 12 persons for allegedly protesting against a quarantine facility in Bhayander. On March 19, a vacant Mhada building in New Golden Nest, Bhayander (East), was identified for quarantining suspected Covid-19 patients. Ward officer Narendra Chavan said protesters prevented transport of medical equipment.
