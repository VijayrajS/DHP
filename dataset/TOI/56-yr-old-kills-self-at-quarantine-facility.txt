56-yr old kills self at quarantine facility
2020-04-07
Rudrapur: A 56-year-old man, who was admitted to the quarantine facility at Uttarakhand Institute of Rural Development (UIRD) in Rudrapur last week, committed suicide by hanging from a hook in the ceiling on Monday.

The incident came to light when the staff at UIRD went to give him dinner but the man did not open the door.

The victim was a resident of Pilibhit in UP and was admitted to the quarantine facility on March 31 by Khatima police after he showed symptoms of novel coronavirus. Police officials said that they are investigating and matter and trying to ascertain if there were any other reasons behind the suicide.
