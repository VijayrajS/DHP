Covid-19: Rlys starts passenger screening, UP roadways sanitizes buses
2020-03-18
AGRA: Railway kick-started screening of passengers at Agra cantt railway station. A long queue of passengers waited for their turn to get screened by railway medical staff for Covid-19 infection.


Coronavirus in India live updates


Meanwhile, Agra region of UP roadways distributed sanitising kit to their employees in order to get their fleet of buses cleaned.
