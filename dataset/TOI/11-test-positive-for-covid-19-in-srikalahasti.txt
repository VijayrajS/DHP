11 test positive for Covid-19 in Srikalahasti
2020-04-20
TIRUPATI: Eleven people, including eight government staff, were tested positive in Srikalahasti on Sunday, taking the overall number of Covid-19 cases in Chittoor district to 35. In Anantapur district, three new cases were reported.

“The new cases were detected through random sampling carried out in red zone. The families of the government employees were put under quarantine,” said Chittoor district collector Narayana Bharat Gupta. The collector said inter-mandal and inter-district movement will be curtailed from Monday.
