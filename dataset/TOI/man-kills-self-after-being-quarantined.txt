Man kills self after being quarantined
2020-04-26
Sidhi: A 30-year-old farm labourer who was quarantined at a school in Sidhi district, allegedly committed suicide, police said on Friday. The man, who belongs to the Baiga tribe, was found hanging from a tree near the quarantine facility at Dubri Kala village on Thursday, superintendent of police R S Bevanshi said. As per preliminary investigation, the man did not wish to be kept in isolation and wanted to be with his children. The deceased, had returned to the village from Sagar. PTI
