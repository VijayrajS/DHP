Covid positive migrant workers get hygiene kits in Kheri
2020-05-20
Bareilly: With the support of local residents, CMO, Kheri, Dr Manoj Agarwal on Tuesday, distributed person hygiene kits to all migrant workers who were tested Covid-19 positive in Kheri district.There are 30 active patients in Kheri and 29 of them are from poor background.Since maintaining personal hygiene was a real challenge for them, the CMO sought support from the district magistrate and contacted people who voluntarily wanted to make contribution.The cost of each kit is nearly Rs 800 and it contains a bucket, mug, sanitizer, inner-wear, soap, towel etc.
