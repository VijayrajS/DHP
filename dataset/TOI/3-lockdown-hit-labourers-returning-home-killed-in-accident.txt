Three lockdown-hit labourers returning home killed in accident
2020-03-30
DAVANAGERE: Three construction labourers, left jobless in Bengaluru due to the Covid-19 lockdown and forced to return to their homes in Kalaburagi district, were killed after a speeding SUV hit the tractor in which they were travelling near KR Halli Gate on NH 4 in Chitradurga district on Sunday. The accident left 13 other labourers injured.


The deceased Mounesh, Sakkappa and Mahadevappa were residents of Aranur village in Jeevargi taluk. Ten of the injured were shifted to Hiriyur Taluk Hospital and the rest to Chitradurga District Hospital. Hiriyur police have booked the absconding SUV driver.
