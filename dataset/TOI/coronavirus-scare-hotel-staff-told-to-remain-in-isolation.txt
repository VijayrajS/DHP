Coronavirus scare: Hotel staff told to remain in isolation
2020-03-05
JODHPUR: Fourteen staff members of a hotel here have been advised to remain in isolation and sample of swab and blood of one of them has been taken for examination after the report that the COVID-19 infected Italian couple had travelled Jodhpur and had stayed here on February 25. 

According to the state government, the couple as part of an Italian tourist group of 23 had landed in Jodhpur on 25 February and had stayed in the room number 621 of the hotel 'Zone by the Park'. An extensive disinfection and fumigation exercise has been carried out in the hotel. The entire floor where the tourists had been accommodated was sealed on Wednesday.
