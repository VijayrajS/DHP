Covid +ve woman gives birth to boy
2020-05-09
Hyderabad: Gandhi Hospital doctors performed caesarean section, helping a 27-year-old coronapositive woman deliver a baby boy on Friday. The entire family of the woman was infected with Covid-19. The baby will be tested for coronavirus on Saturday. According to hospital superintendent Dr Raja Rao, the mother and child are doing well.The condition of the baby is being closely monitored. 

The surgery was headed by Dr Mahalaxmi. Incidentally, this is the first time that a Covid-19 positive woman delivered a baby in the state.TNN
