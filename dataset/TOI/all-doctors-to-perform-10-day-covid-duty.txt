All doctors to perform 10-day Covid duty
2020-06-12
Ahmedabad: In a notification on Thursday, Dr MM Prabhakar, OSD, Medicity, mentioned that based on new guidelines issued by the Government of India, all officers and workers would have to perform 10-day duty at Covid-19 hospital. The duty will be followed by five-day break from work, followed by seven days of non-Covid duty. The officer/ doctor will again be deployed at Covid-19 hospital for 10-day and so on. ‘Respective departments will send the duty list to the medical superintendent, RMO and OSD Dr MD Gajjar,’ mentioned the notification.
