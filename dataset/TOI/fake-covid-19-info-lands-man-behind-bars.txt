Fake Covid-19 information lands man behind bars
2020-04-04
LUDHIANA: The police arrested a 22-year-old resident of Janakpuri on Friday afternoon after he called up on 112 police helpline and said that three people have died in Mangli Neechi village due to coronavirus. The information was later found to be false. 


Ishwar Colony police post in-charge ASI Surjit Singh said, “After receiving the call of Sandeep Bhat, 22, the police investigated the claims and found them to be false. We have lodged an FIR and arrested Sandeep.”
