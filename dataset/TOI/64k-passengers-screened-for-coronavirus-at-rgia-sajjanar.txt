64k passengers screened for coronavirus at RGIA: Sajjanar
2020-03-16
Hyderabad: Passengers who are above 60 years of age and suffering from respiratory problems and diabetes or returned from any Covid-19 affected countries, have been quarantined for 14 days, said Cyberabad police commissioner VC Sajjanar after reviewing screening arrangements at Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (RGIA) on Sunday.

“As many as 64,000 passengers arriving from China, Iran, South Korea, Italy, Spain, Germany, and France have been screened at RGIA so far,” he said

“Around 200 doctors and medical staff with sufficient ambulances are working 24x7, while around 200 police staff and CISF are providing security at RGIA,” Cyberabad police said in an official statement.
