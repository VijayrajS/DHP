At 211, Mandya has most active Covid-19 cases in Karnataka
2020-05-24
MANDYA: Covid-19 cases in Mandya are increasing rapidly, with 28 fresh cases being reported on Saturday. It has taken the total tally to 237 in the district. The number is likely to go up higher.

According to deputy commissioner Venkatesh, out of a total 237 Covid-19 cases, 26 people have been cured and discharged from hospital. As of now 211 active cases remain, which is the highest in any district in Karnataka. Bengaluru has 124 active cases while in other district active cases are below 100.
