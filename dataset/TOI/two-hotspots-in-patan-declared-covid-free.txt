Two hotspots in Patan declared Covid-free
2020-05-11
Palanpur: Tammana Society in Siddhpur and adjoining Nendra village have been declared Covid-19 free in accordance to the fresh guidelines issued by the central government, said district development officer, DK Parekh of Patan. 

Once a Covid hotspot, Nendra had registered 12 positive cases, while one resident of in Tammana Society had succumbed. At present, Patan district has only five patients in the isolation ward, while an equal number have recovered fully.

In another development, Aravali collector Amrutesh Aurangabadkar has declared complete lockdown of Modasa, the district headquarters, from May 11 till May 17. However, medicine, milk, fuel and LPG cylinders will be allowed.
