124 arrested for flouting lockdown order
2020-04-01
RAJKOT: A total of 124 people were arrested for allegedly flouting the coronavirus lockdown order in Rajkot city and Jamnagar district on Tuesday. According to police sources, in Rajkot city, 34 people were caught venturing out of their homes and in Jamnagar district, 84 people were arrested for the same offence.
