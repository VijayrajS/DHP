Nashik: Four new cases take Covid-19 tally to 516
2020-06-12
NASHIK: The number of Covid-19 positive cases in Nashik city has increased to 516 with addition of four new cases on Thursday.


The city currently has 294 active cases and 194 patients have so far recovered and got discharged from the hospital. The number of positive cases crossed the 500-mark on Wednesday with 25 people testing positive for the contagion.

The city has recorded 464 cases in just last three weeks. On Thursday, two more people succumbed to the disease, taking the death toll to 26. The first case of Covid-19 in Nashik city was reported on April 6 and till May 20, the total number of cases stood at 48. It took around 50 days to reach100 cases, but cases doubled in just seven days. Now, the city is recording 100 cases in every 3-4 days for the past 12 days. tnn
