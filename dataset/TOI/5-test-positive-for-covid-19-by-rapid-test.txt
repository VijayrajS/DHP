5 test positive for Covid-19 by rapid test
2020-04-19
Jaipur: Five persons tested positive for Covid-19 on Saturday by rapid test, which was introduced in the state on Friday. Health department claimed that Rajasthan is the first state to use rapid test for diagnosis of Covid-19. The test was used on 1078 persons in 15 different areas of the Walled City. According to the health department, five persons were found infected with the virus with use of rapid test. One each person from Ramganj, Moti Katla, Chaura Rasta, Govind Nagar and Laxminarayanpuri. The health department officials said that the test is not confirmatory test. Those tested positive in rapid test have been isolated and their swab samples will be transported to SMS medical college's microbiology department. TNN
