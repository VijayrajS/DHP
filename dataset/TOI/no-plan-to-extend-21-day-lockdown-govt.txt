No plan to extend 21-day lockdown: Govt
2020-03-31
Cabinet secretary Rajiv Gauba on Monday denied speculation that the government may extend the 21-day lockdown after April 14. Expressing surprise at some reports to this effect, Gauba confirmed there were no such plans. “There are rumours & media reports, claiming that the Government will extend the #Lockdown21 when it expires. The Cabinet Secretary has denied these reports, and stated that they are baseless,” the PIB tweet said.
