Mangaluru: One more coronavirus patient gets discharged from hospital
2020-04-15
MANGALURU: One more Covid-19 positive patient was discharged from District Wenlock Hospital in Mangaluru on Tuesday. With this, the number of active cases in the district reduced to three from 12. 


The 49-year-old man, who has a travel history of visiting Dubai, was discharged on Tuesday following his report tested negative for the infection. He had reached his village in Puttur taluk on March 21. He was admitted to Wenlock Hospital with symptoms on April 1 after he was tested positive for Covi-19. The patient was discharged after samples sent on April 12 and 13 tested negative.

In Dakshina Kannada, no positive cases were reported in the past 10 days.
