Covid-19 suspect woman flees isolation ward, nabbed
2020-04-14
Rajkot: A woman along with her child fled from the isolation ward of the Rajkot Civil Hospital on Monday evening. However, the woman identified as Kashmir Ajmeri, was nabbed from her residence in Thorala by the officials of Thorala police station in a matter of minutes. “Ajmeri was admitted to the isolation ward of the hospital as a suspected Covid-19 case. We nabbed her along with her child and brought them back to the hospital,” said an official from Thorala police station.
