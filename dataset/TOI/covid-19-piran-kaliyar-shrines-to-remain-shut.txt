Covid-19: Piran Kaliyar shrines to remain shut
2020-03-21
Piran Kaliyar shrines closedRoorkee: Uttarakhand Waqf Board (UWB) on Friday ordered closure of the three Piran Kaliyar-based sufi shrines — Dargah Sabir Pak, Dargah Imaam Sahab and Kilkilee Sahab — with immediate effect to avoid mass gatherings in view of the Covid-19 threat. “Stay of outsiders has been strictly prohibited inside the shrine. Pilgrims are being vacated from there and proper sanitation arrangements are being ensured inside the premises,” said Ahmed Iqbal, chief executive officer of UWB .
