Covid-19: ISB stops classes
2020-03-18
HYDERABAD: Indian School of Business (ISB) on Tuesday said it has suspended classes at its Hyderabad and Mohali campuses as a safety measure against coronavirus. 

“As precautionary measure and keeping in view the safety of everyone, classes have been suspended and all the students have been advised to vacate the campuses by Wednesday, March 18,” ISB said in a statement.
