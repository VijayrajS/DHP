Four booked for violating lockdown
2020-05-23
Hyderabad: A case has been booked against four people for operating a swimming pool in violation of lockdown rules at Rein Bazar on Friday. Police raided the pool after a tip-off from credible sources and booked the four under section 188 (Disobedience to order duly promulgated by the public servant) and relevant sections of the Epidemic Act and Disaster Management Act.
