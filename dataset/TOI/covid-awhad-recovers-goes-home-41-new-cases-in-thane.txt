Covid+ NCP leader and cabinet minister Jitendra Awhad recovers, goes home; 41 new cases in Thane
2020-05-11
THANE: NCP leader and cabinet minister Jitendra Awhad announced his recovery and discharge from a private hospital in Mumbai on Sunday. 


He tweeted his gratitude to the hospital staff, and also thanked his family, colleagues and CM Uddhav Thackeray for their support. "I will take a brief period for myself and following this, will return to serve residents from my constituency," he said in a series of tweets.
