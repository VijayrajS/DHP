Ahmedabad woman booked after posting lockdown violation video
2020-05-02
AHMEDABAD: A Vastrapur woman who uploaded her own lockdown-violation video on social media was booked by police on Friday. She had posted a video of herself passing by the Vastrapur police station 

According to the FIR, the woman giggles while passing by the police station and she is apparently heard saying that she does not fear police. 

On the basis of her social media accounts, she was identified as a resident of Goyal Intercity. Vastrapur police have booked her under IPC Section 188 for disobeying a government notification. Charges under the Epidemic Act have been invoked as well.
