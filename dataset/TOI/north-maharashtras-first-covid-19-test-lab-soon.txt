North Maharashtra's first Covid-19 test lab soon
2020-03-18
NASHIK: The Dhule-based Shri Bhausaheb Hire Government Medical College and Hospital (SBHGMCH) has initiated the process to establish a viral research and diagnostic lab on its premises to carry out real-time (RT) PCR tests for Covid-19 on a war-footing. 


The laboratory, the maiden one in North Maharashtra, is coming up on the directives of the ICMR and the state government. Top officials of the institute, led by dean Dr Nagsen Ramraje and hospital superintendent Rajkumar Suryavanshi, have prepared a blueprint for the project.
