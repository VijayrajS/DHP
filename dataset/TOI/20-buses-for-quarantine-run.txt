20 buses for quarantine run
2020-03-19
On Wednesday evening, two senior deputy municipal commissioners Om Prakash and K B Thakkar were rushed to the city airport for setting up transportation facility and to spruce up fever screening facility at the airport . “We ask flyers to self-declare their travel history, any symptoms, and if they took paracetamol before leaving the aircraft,” said an AMC official. Twenty BRTS and AMTS buses have been deployed to take flyers with Covid-19 symptoms to a nearby quarantine.
