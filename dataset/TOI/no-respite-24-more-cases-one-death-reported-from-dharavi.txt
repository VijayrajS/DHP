No respite: 24 more Covid-19 cases, one death reported from Dharavi
2020-05-10
MUMBAI: Dharavi reported 25 new Covid-19 cases on Saturday, which included a death. The cases now stand at 833, including 27 deaths. Of the total patients being reported from Dharavi so far, 222 have been discharged. 

BMC is increasing institutional quarantine facilities following instructions from the civic chief to isolate those unable to isolate themselves. 

Mahim reported five new cases, taking the total there to 112. The cases from Mahim include five deaths. 

Dadar reported 18 new cases, taking the total to 107, including five deaths. Seventeen of the positive cases from Dadar have been discharged. Richa Pinto
