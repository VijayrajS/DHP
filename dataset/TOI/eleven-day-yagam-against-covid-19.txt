Telangana: Eleven-day yagam against coronavirus
2020-03-19
Sarada Peetham began its 11-day amrutha pashupat yagam for visaha jwara peedhara on Wednesday. The peetham claimed the ritual would prevent the spread of coronavirus. The yagam is being conducted by ritual experts and rutviks under the supervision of Sarada Peetham Seer Swaroopanandendra at the Peetham in Chinnamushidiwada. 


According to Uttaradhikari of the peetham, Swamy Swatmanandendra, the country is passing through a bad phase as a result of kalasarpa dosha due to planetary positions. Uttaradhikari said the yaga would ensure the health and wellbeing of all citizens.
