Coronavirus: 13k screened at city airport
2020-02-15
Jaipur: Twelve thousand nine hundred ninety two passengers have been screened at Jaipur International Airport in the wake of coronavirus alert coming in from 84 international flights till Friday.In a release by Directorate Medical & Health Services, it was informed that screening of all passengers has been going on since January 28. Five doctors and five paramedical nurses have been deputed at airport. Samples of 80 patients were collected and all 80 were found negative. Additional chief secretary (medical and health) Rohit Kumar Singh issued directions to health officials to remain sensitive and asked them to identify tourists from China and get their thermal screening done.
