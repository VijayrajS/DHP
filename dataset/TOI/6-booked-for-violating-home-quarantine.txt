6 booked for violating home quarantine
2020-03-30
Kolhapur: The city police on Sunday booked another six people for violating the 14-day home quarantine norm.

According to police, three cases have been registered with Shahapur police station, 2 with Shivajinagar police station and one at Radhanagari police station.

“In all these cases, the suspects either have a travel history or they came in contact with people having travel history. They were asked not to leave their homes and their hands were stamped. Despite this they were found loitering in the market area and on major roads in the jurisdiction of respective police stations. They were detained after police from respective police stations got information about their movement,” a senior police officer said.

Police have booked them under sections 188 (Disobedience to order duly promulgated by public servant), 291 (Continuance of nuisance after injunction to discontinue), 269 (Negligent act likely to spread infection of disease dangerous to life) and 270 (Malignant act likely to spread infection of disease dangerous to life) of the Indian Penal Code.

Meanwhile, police also continued their drive against motorists coming on road without any reason and have seized as many as 202 two-wheelers from across the district on Sunday. “The drive will continue till the lockdown is lifted,” said police.
