Gujarat: 38 linked to Delhi event quarantined
2020-04-03
SURAT: The Bharuch district administration has successfully traced another 24 individuals from the district who came in contact with those who attended the Tablighi Jamat event at Nizamuddin.


On Thursday, police and the health authorities scouted out the 24 who had visited Bhavnagar and Tamil Nadu and established contact with the people there who attended the Nizamuddin event. About 38 people were traced from the district and have been strictly asked to stay under home quarantine.

A senior health officer said, “We have ordered home quarantine for all the 24 people. As of now, a total of 62 people are under home quarantine in the district.”
