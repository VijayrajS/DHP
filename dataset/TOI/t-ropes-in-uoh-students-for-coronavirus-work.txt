Telangana ropes in University of Hyderabad students for coronavirus work
2020-04-10
HYDERABAD: Students pursuing public health at the University of Hyderabad (UoH) have been drafted by the state government to carry out epidemiological field work in various districts as part of its containment strategy against the spread of Covid-19.

According to a press release issued by UoH, 15 students pursuing master of public health course have been issued orders by district authorities to participate in active and passive surveillance teams. In addition, four research scholars and another 30 students have submitted their credentials. TNN
