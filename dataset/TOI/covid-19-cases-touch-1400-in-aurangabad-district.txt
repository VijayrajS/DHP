Covid-19 cases touch 1,400 in Aurangabad district
2020-05-29
AURANGABAD: Covid-19 cases in Aurangabad district touched the 1400 mark on Thursday with the addition of 14 fresh cases. The district has registered 65 coronvirus-related deaths so far.


The new cases diagnosed on Thursday include one each from Baijipura, Rahemaniya Colony, Misarwadi, Waluj Mahanagar-I, Bajajnagar, Sanjay Nagar, Shahgunj, Hussain Colony, Kailas Nagar, Rokadiya, Osmanpura, Itkheda, City Chowk, Nath Nagar, Balaji Nagar, Sai Nagar Cidco N-6 , Kareem Colony, Roshan Gate, Anguri Baug, Tanhaji Chowk, Balaji Nagar and Hudco N-11, two each from the Railway Station area, Cidco N-6, Naralibaug, Sambhaji Colony Cidco N-6 and Jai Bhawani Nagar, three from Hanuman Colony and four from Cidco N-4 and Hamalwadi. 

Arvind Gaikwad, nodal officer for media interaction at the Government Medical College and Hospital (GMCH) said that as on Thursday evening, 76 Covid-19 patients were admitted to the hospital, of which, seven were in critical condition.
