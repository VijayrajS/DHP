3 walk from Kasaragod to Nagenahalli, quarantined
2020-04-24
Mysuru: Three people who came to Nagenahalli on Thursday morning, by walking, have been home quarantined . Locals alerted the taluk health department regarding their arrival in the village. 

They are reported to have come to the village, by walking from Kasaragod in Kerala, via Mangaluru in Dakshina Kannada. As both these areas are Covid-19 hotspots, locals alerted the health department about their arrival. 

Taluk health officer Dr T Ravikumar came to the village, after being intimated by locals. Even though the three individuals appeared to be healthy, the authorities asked them to remain in home quarantine for the next 14 days.
