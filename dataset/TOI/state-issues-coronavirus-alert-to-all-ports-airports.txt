State issues coronavirus alert to all ports, airports
2020-02-01
Vijayawada: The state government has issued an alert to all ports and airports in the state in the wake of the coronavirus outbreak in China. The state has also appointed nodal officers in every district to screen suspected patients for the virus.

Health minister Alla Nani held a review meeting with the higher officials on Friday and directed them to be ready to deal with any positive cases that are found. He told officials to create quarantine zones in every hospital and to procure masks and other equipment needed. 

An alert was sounded at all ports and airports to scan all passengers arriving from foreign countries . Nodal officers will coordinate with various departments in virus screening. tnn
