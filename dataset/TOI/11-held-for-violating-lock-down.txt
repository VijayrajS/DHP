Ahmedabad: 11 held for violating lockdown
2020-05-09
AHMEDADBAD: Narol police on Friday arrested 11 people for violating the section 144 which prohibits the gathering of more than four people at one place and also for the violation of the lock-down order. 

The cops said they spotted a group of people standing near a society in Narol. However, upon seeing the cops’ vehicle, the people started running inside the society. “We chased the group and arrested eleven of them,” said an official from Narol police station.
