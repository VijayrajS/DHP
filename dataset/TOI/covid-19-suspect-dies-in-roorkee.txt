Covid-19 suspect dies in Roorkee
2020-06-10
Roorkee: An 18-year-old boy, who was admitted to Roorkee's Civil Hospital after displaying coronavirus-like symptoms, died on Tuesday. 

According to Dr Sanjay Kansal, chief medical superintendent of the Civil Hospital, the victim, a resident of Prem Nagar locality, was put in the isolation ward of the hospital on Monday. The victim was suffering from throat infection and coughing for weeks. Prior to being admitted to the government facility, he had seen private doctors as well.

“He was brought to our facility on Monday and we collected his samples for Covid-19 test the same day. However, he collapsed today. His report will likely come by Wednesday,” said Dr Kansal.
