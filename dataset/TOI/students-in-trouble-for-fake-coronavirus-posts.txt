Kadapa students in trouble for fake coronavirus posts
2020-03-07
TIRUPATI: Four students of an engineering college in Kadapa are in the dock for putting up false information on coronavirus on social media platforms to create fear among people. 


The students were suspend by the private college’s management and a nuisance case was lodged with the police.

The second and third year students posted a television grab and wrote ‘Breaking news: Corona virus scare in Kadapa’ on it, in an attempt to spread panic and threaten fellow students. 

Taking serious note of the mischief, the principal suspend them from college and hostel and also filed a complaint at Chennur Police Station in Kadapa. The management appealed to students and staff not to believe such fake news.
