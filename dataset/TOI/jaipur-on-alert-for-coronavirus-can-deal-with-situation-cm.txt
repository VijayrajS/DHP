Jaipur on alert for coronavirus, can deal with situation: CM
2020-03-05
JAIPUR: Appealing the people not to worry, chief minister Ashok Gehlot on Wednesday said that the state government is alert and well equipped to deal with coronavirus (Covid-19). He said the government will take all measures to prevent the disease from spreading in the state. 


While reviewing the situation in the state with health minister Raghu Sharma, chief secretary D B Gupta, additional chief secretary Rohit Kumar Singh and other health department officials and medical education department officials at his residence on Wednesday said that advisory should be issued to all the collectors of the districts visited by the group of Italian tourist, among them two were tested positive for Covid-19 in Sawai Man Singh Hospital.
