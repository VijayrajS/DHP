Cured patient moved to quarantine facility
2020-04-10
Panaji: One person who was declared cured of Covid-19 on Wednesday was transferred to a quarantine facility in North Goa on Thursday, while six patients are still under treatment at ESI hospital, Margao.

As per protocol, the person is required to stay in at a monitored quarantine facility before final discharge. 

Chief minister Pramod Sawant said the condition of the other six patients was improving.

There are 12 patients admitted to the isolation ward at Goa Medical College and Hospital. It includes the four who were admitted on Thursday. As many as 45 suspects were placed under home quarantine on Thursday. Of the 24 samples sent for testing, nine have tested negative for the virus, while 15 reports are awaited.
