Two more Covid-19 cases in Tapi district
2020-05-24
Surat: Two Covid-19 positive cases were detected in Tapi district on Saturday. This has taken total number of coronavirus cases in the district to six. Dr. Harshad Patel, chief district health officer, Tapi, said, Rushi Bagde, 37, a resident of Songadh, who had come from Limbayat area of Surat on May 20, has tested positive for the virus.
