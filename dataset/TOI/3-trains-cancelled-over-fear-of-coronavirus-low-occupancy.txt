Three trains cancelled over fear of coronavirus, low occupancy
2020-03-20
DEHRADUN: The Railways on Thursday cancelled operations of three trains operating in the state from March 20 to March 31, in view of the growing threat of coronavirus as well as low occupancy. 


A communique from the Railways said that the cancelled trains are 12401/02 Kota-Dehradun Nanda Devi Express, 01701/02 Jabalpur-Haridwar special train, and 12091/92 Dehradun-Kathgodam Naini Jan Shatabdi. 

“All passengers having tickets in these trains will not be charged any cancellation fees. They will get a 100 per cent refund,” said an official of the Indian Railways
