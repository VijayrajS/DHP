Telugu groups in US counter Covid-19 impact
2020-03-18
Hyderabad: The Indian embassy and consulates in the US are reaching out to local Telugu associations to mitigate the impact of the coronavirus outbreak there. 

Several organisations have launched helpline numbers to help the community.

Jayanth Challa, Board of Trustee, American Telugu Association confirmed that the embassy had reached out to the organization over the weekend. “ATA had a conference call with Anurag Kumar Sharma, minister of community affairs, Indian Embassy to assist Indian students in the US impacted by Covid-19. 

Speaking about measures being taken, he said, “Indian students will be given help in finding accommodation and provided medical assistance.” ATA has launched a helpline and identified coordinators in major cities to help students. “We are compiling a list of Indian families willing to accommodate students,” said Challa.
