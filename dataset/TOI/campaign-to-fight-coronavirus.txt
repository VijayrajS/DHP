Campaign to fight coronavirus
2020-05-05
HYDERABAD: Tata Trusts, a philanthropic organisation, has committed Rs 500 crore to the fight against Covid-19, collaborating with state governments to provide them with PPEs among other items. The exercise intends to create awareness, engage and educate rural masses. 

The campaign ‘5 kadam, Corona mukt jeevan’, ranges from video messages, short animation videos and infographics to audio messages and SMS based messaging.
