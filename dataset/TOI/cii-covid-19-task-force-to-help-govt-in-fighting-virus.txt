CII-Telangana Covid-19 task force to help government in fighting virus
2020-04-02
HYDERABAD: The Telangana chapter of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) on Wednesday announced the setting up of a COVID Action Task Force as part of efforts to battle the dreaded virus.

The Telangana CII taskforce comes even as the apex industry body has set up a special COVID-19 Relief & Rehabilitation Fund to provide relief and support to various stakeholders including the healthcare workers on the frontline and the community at large.
