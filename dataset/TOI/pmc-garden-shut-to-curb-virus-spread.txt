PMC garden shut to curb coronavirus spread
2020-03-12
PUNE: The civic body has decided to shut down the Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule garden in the vicinity of the housing society where three residents tested positive for coronavirus. 



Coronavirus outbreak live updates
The decision was taken as a preventive measure following instructions from the health department to prevent the spread of the virus. The staffers said the garden was very popular among residents of nearby areas and people of different ages visited it on a daily basis. 

“Many people came to the garden on Wednesday morning but we did not allow them to enter the premises. Entry will be barred for the next few days. The gardener and other staff members have been instructed suitably. We have put a board on the entry gate,” said a staff member.
