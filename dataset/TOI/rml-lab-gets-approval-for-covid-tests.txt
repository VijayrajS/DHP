RML lab gets approval for Covid tests
2020-03-28
Lucknow-based RML Mehrotra Pathology will be the first private sector lab in UP to conduct coronavirus testing. The paper work in this regard is underway, officials said, adding that now state has nine labs for Covid-19 tests. Permission has been granted by the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR). Secretary to CM Alok Kumar said: “Three proposals were sent from UP, but permission was granted only to one.” He added that 29 private labs in the country have been allowed by ICMR to conduct Covid-19 tests.
