Three more test positive, take coronavirus infected to 28 in Rajasthan
2020-03-23
JAIPUR/JODHPUR: Three more persons tested positive for Covid-19 in the state on Sunday. One each positive case was reported from the two curfew affected districts of Jhunjhunu and Bhilwara, while a third was reported from Jodhpur. A total of 28 persons have been diagnosed with Covid-19 so far in the state. However, three of them have cured of the disease.


In Bhilwara, one more paramedical staff tested positive for Covid-19, who worked in the same hospital where 12 other hospital staff had been diagnosed with the disease. In Jhunjhunu, one person, who returned from Dubai and tested positive for the disease.

A 39-year-old, Jodhpur resident, who travelled by a train from Delhi to Jaipur, was tested positive for Covid-19 on Sunday. He had recently travelled to Turkey, from where he returned on March 18. 

There are chances that he might have infected others who had travelled with him in the train.
