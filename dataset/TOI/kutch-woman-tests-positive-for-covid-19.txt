Kutch woman tests positive for Covid-19
2020-03-22
Rajkot: A 59-year-old woman from Kutch tested positive of coronavirus on Saturday evening taking the total positive cases to 14 in the state. The woman is a resident of Lakhpat taluka of Kutch. She had recently returned from Mecca, said Kutch district officials.District development officer (DDO) Prabhav Joshi told TOI, “The woman tested positive and her condition is stable. Her husband has tested negative and her 12 close contacts have been shifted to quarantine facility.” According to sources total 56 people have been asked to remain under isolation for next 14 days and are under observation. The woman had arrived from Mecca on March 15 and was under observation since then..
