Restrain police from seizing vehicles during lockdown: AIMIM to HC
2020-04-30
Charminar: The AIMIM has called on Chief Justice of Telangana asking for restrain on the police department seizing further vehicles of people during the lockdown.

Syed Taraq Quadri told TOI that a letter was written to the chief justice saying that citizens were facing hardship in procuring ration, milk and medicines and step only to meet these needs. 

“In the process, not only are their vehicles being seized, but they are also being beaten. Although Hyderabad police commissioner has appealed to his subordinates to spare the lathy and be, ground realities are different,’’ said AIMIM president. - Omer bin Taher
