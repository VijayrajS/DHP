Coronavirus: Flyers from Italy & Iran to be screened in Mumbai airport
2020-02-27
With coronavirus epidemic spreading, Mumbai airport on Wednesday said it will screen passengers arriving from Italy and Iran also. India has advised its citizens to “refrain from non-essential travel to Korea, Iran and Italy,” the government said in a statement on Wednesday .

Meanwhile, Vistara said it will cancel 54 flights to Bangkok and Singapore next month.
