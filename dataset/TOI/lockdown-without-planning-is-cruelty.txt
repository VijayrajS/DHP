‘Lockdown without planning is cruelty’
2020-03-30
Hyderabad: AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi has branded the 21-day nationwide lockdown as cruelty. 

“State governments of UP, WB & Bihar are doing nothing to aid these stranded migrants. You cannot “play it by the ear” with people’s lives. A lockdown without thinking about the welfare of India’s vast majority is plain cruelty,” tweeted Owaisi. Reacting to migration of workers from Delhi, he questioned if Telanagana chief minister K Chandrashekar Rao should help the migrant workers with transport facilities. He also alleged that state governments of Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal and Bihar not providing aid to stranded migrants. TNN
