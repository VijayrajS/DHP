Two test negative for Covid-19 infection
2020-03-18
Rajkot: Two patients admitted in Rajkot Civil Hospital and a private hospital tested negative for nCOVID-19 infection even as the district administration has started surveillance of close contacts of people who returned from abroad in the last two-and-a-half months. According to health department sources, officials will visit relatives of patients who were under observation for 14 days for acute respiration symptoms.Nearly 290 people came to Rajkot from foreign countries in last two-and-a-half months and medical history of nearly 700 people will be checked.Meanwhile, the railways has suspended operation of Jamnagar-Bandra Humsafar Express upto March 31 in wake of rising number of cases in Maharashtra.
