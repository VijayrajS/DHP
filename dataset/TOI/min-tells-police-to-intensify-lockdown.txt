Minister tells police to intensify lockdown
2020-04-17
HYDERABAD: Police will further tighten lockdown in Habeeb Nagar, Asif Nagar, Humayun Nagar, Golconda and Tolichowki, upon direction from minister KT Rama Rao. “Do what you think is necessary to implement lockdown effectively,” the minister told joint commissioner A R Srinivas.
