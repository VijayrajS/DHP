Five arrested for hosting dinner party in Karnataka during lockdown
2020-04-03
DHARWAD: Dharwad Town police on Thursday arrested five men from Bara Imam Galli for allegedly hosting a dinner party for more than 30 people in defiance of the lockdown orders.


The accused Irfan, Ashfaq, Wasim, Arbaz and Aslam have been charged under sections 188, 269 and 270 of the IPC, said assistant commissioner of police Anusha.

A video of the dinner in Bara Imam Galli where mutton biryani was served on Wednesday went viral on social media, prompting police to take action against the organisers.
