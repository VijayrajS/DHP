Third positive Covid-19 case from Gadag
2020-04-19
HUBBALLI: Gadag district reported its third positive case on Saturday, a 42-year-old man(P-370) from Ranganwadi area. 

This is the third case of the district, all the positive cases reported from the same area Ranganwadi. The administration said that P-370 is the secondary contact of P-304, a lady who tested positive to the infection on Thursday.
