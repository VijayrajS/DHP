BHOPAL: A 75-year-old corona-positive woman died during treatment at a government hospital in Morena. She was not only visually challenged, but was also suffering from multiple complications. Her funeral was carried out by the local administration in compliance with the guidelines of government of India.

The first Covid-19 positive patients were found in Morena in February second week. Three people, including a couple, who had returned from Dubai, were the first patients.

Later, around 22 people who had come in direct contact with the positive patients were shifted to hospital quarantine, while the entire area where the positive patients lived has been declared a containment zone.
