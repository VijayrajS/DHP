Uttarkashi admin books Covid patient for hiding travel history
2020-05-23
Uttarkashi: A Covid-19 patient in a village in Yamuna Valley region of Uttarkashi district has been charged under attempt to murder for allegedly reaching his home secretly and hiding his travel history from the administration. The revenue police have also booked the family of the accused, who returned from Mumbai and was declared Covid positive on May 20. This is the second such incident in a week in the district.
