5 Mumbai returnees test Covid-19 positive in Kalaburagi
2020-05-29
KALABURAGI: Five persons who returned from Mumbai tested positive for Covid-19 in Kalaburagi on Thursday. 

The infected include three women, a 9-year-old boy and a 3-year old boy. 

They were in the quarantine centre and after testing positive they were shifted to the ESIC hospital. 

With this, the number of active cases in Kalaburagi has gone up to 108.
