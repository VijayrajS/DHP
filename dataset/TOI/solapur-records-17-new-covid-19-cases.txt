Solapur records 17 new Covid-19 cases
2020-05-17
Kolhapur: Solapur registered 17 fresh Covid-19 cases on Saturday. With this, the total number of active cases in the district has gone up to 360. While there have been 24 casualties, 113 patients have recovered.

Of the 17 patients, 10 are men and seven are women. According to the official information released by the district administration, 17 of 148 people tested positive. District guardian minister Datta Bharne has appealed to the citizens not to panic. He said that number of cases was on the rise as the administration had increased the number of tests for the suspected people in the containment zones. tnn
