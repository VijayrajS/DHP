249 check into facility quarantine
2020-05-11
Panaji: The virology laboratory of Goa Medical College (GMC) and hospital, Bambolim, on Sunday tested 324 samples for Covid-19 and all tested negative for the virus. On Sunday, 249 contacts were lodged in facility quarantine.

Three suspected cases (patients showing symptoms) were admitted to the isolation ward on Sunday and their test results are awaited. A total of six suspects are now admitted to the isolation ward of GMC, and one is under observation at the North Goa district hospital, Mapusa.
