Unemployment not virus will kill us, say migrant workers
2020-03-31
Determined migrant workers fear for their jobs more than the coronavirus outbreak, lockdown or the sealing of inter-state borders even as they make the backbreaking trek of over 1,000 kilometres, traversing three to four states, with their family and children, find out Siddhartha Dutta and Manasij Bhattacharjee.
