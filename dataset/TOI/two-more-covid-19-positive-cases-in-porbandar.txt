Two more Covid-19 positive cases in Porbandar
2020-04-02
Rajkot: Two more people in Porbandar tested Covid-19 positive, taking the total number of infected persons to three in the district. According to the district administration, all the positive cases are of the same group. A 12-year-old girl and a 40-year-man tested positive on Wednesday. The girl is the daughter of the woman who tested positive on March 29. The man also belongs to the same group that came to Porbandar from Dubai a few days before the woman tested positive. While the elder daughter has been kept in isolation, the younger daughter tested positive on Wednesday. The man who tested positive also came from Dubai.
