After Covid report leaks, DC orders probe
2020-04-24
Hubballi: Dharwad deputy commissioner Deepa Cholan has ordered an internal inquiry after details of Covid-19 patients were leaked much before the official announcement. Her confidential letter to police commissioner R Dileep, seeking details of patients was widely circulated on social media on Thursday. Deepa said the administration is trying to find out who leaked the information and made public the name of Covid-19 patients. She said appropriate action will be initiated against the guilty.
