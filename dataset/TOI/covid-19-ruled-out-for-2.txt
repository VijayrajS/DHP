Two persons test negative for Coronavirus in Tirupati
2020-03-09
TIRUPATI: Two persons who returned from abroad and admitted to the isolation ward at SVRR Government General Hospital in Tirupati tested negative for coronavirus on Sunday. 

The swab samples of the two persons who returned from Singapore and Germany tested negative at the Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences in Tirupati, said the medical superintendent of SVRR Hospital Dr N V Ramanaiah.

So far no positive case of coronavirus has been reported in the state and the state health department has taken several measures to prevent the spread of the disease.
