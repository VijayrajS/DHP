NER staff to get salary for lockdown period too
2020-03-26
Gorakhpur: Amid corona outbreak and complete lockdown, the outsourced and contractual employees of North Eastern Railways will get salary even when railways has discontinued services till March 31. 

“They will be paid salary without work till the resumption of rail services,” said chief public relation officer Pankaj Singh.

The beneficiary will include all outsourced and contractual employees involved in range of services - onboard catering, pantry, sanitary workers, sanitary workers at station and offices.

All those employees who have been awarded contract on the basis of man power will be covered under the scheme and will be paid salary without work till the resumption of rail services.
