Four more test positive for Covid-19 in Hubballi
2020-05-18
DHARWAD: Four more persons, including a 16-year-old boy, tested positive for Covid-19 in Dharwad district on Sunday. 


With this, the total number of those infected in the district has risen to 26. Of them, seven have been cured and discharged, leaving 19 active cases which are being treated at KIMS Hospital in Hubballi.

The fresh cases pertain to P-1123 (39-year-old man), P-1124 (boy), P-1142 (28-year-old woman) and P-1143 (25-year-old man), who are the residents of Shantinagar, Ayodhya Nagar and Navanagar in Hubballi.

According to an official release, P-1123 had returned from Mumbai, P-1124 was the contact of P-589, and P-1142 and P-1143 had returned from Kolhapur in Maharashtra.
