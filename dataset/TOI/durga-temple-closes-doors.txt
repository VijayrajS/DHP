Covid-19: Durga temple in Vijayawada closes doors
2020-03-20
VIJAYAWADA: Sri Durga Malleswara Swamy Varla Devastanams atop Indrakeeladri shrine in the city will remain closed from Friday morning as per the decision of the state government. The decision came as a part of a preventive measure for controlling the spread of Covid-19.

The trust board of the temple conducted emergency meetings with available members and higher officials on Thursday morning and decided to take stringent measures. Temple trust board chairman Paila Sominaidu proposed to cancel all arjitha sevas in the temple. 

 However, temple authorities said that regular poojas and rituals would continue though the temple remains closed.
