Coronavirus in Lucknow: Air, rail traffic suspended for Janta curfew
2020-03-22
LUCKNOW: Air, rail and road transport services will halt on Sunday to check coronavirus outbreak. Air traffic will remain suspended on Sunday from Amausi Airport to various destinations as a part of Janta Curfew. 


Few domestic flights of all airliners will also remain suspended till March 28, said Sanjay Narayan, officer, special duty, Airports Authority of India. International flights will remain cancelled as part of the national lockdown, he said Railway authorities said 40 mail and express trains originating from Lucknow divisions of North Eastern Railway and Northern Railway will not operate between 4am and 10pm on Sunday in view of Janta Curfew.
