Youth lands in jail for coronavirus ‘prank’
2020-03-26
KOTA: Making up false stories of being infected with coronavirus landed 25-year-old Giriraj Sain in jail under judicial custody on Tuesday in Jhalawar. The youth claimed to have travelled to various places and that he had been infected by the virus. 

He allegedly made up these stories in his neighbourhood ‘for fun’. When health officials arrived, he said he was doing it for fun and didn’t travel any country. Sain was later arrested by the police.
