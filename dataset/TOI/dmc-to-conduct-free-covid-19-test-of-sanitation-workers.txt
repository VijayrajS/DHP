DMC to conduct free Covid-19 test of sanitation workers
2020-06-13
The Dehradun Municipal Corporation has decided to test all its sanitation workers for Covid-19 infection. The check-ups will be conducted at a city-based private pathology lab. The DMC claims that around 3,000 sanitation workers will be tested free-of-cost under this drive. “The DMC will bear the cost of Covid-19 test of sanitation workers. The tests will be done at a private lab,” said Kailash Joshi, chief health officer of DMC.
