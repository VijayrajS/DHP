Covid-19: NSS urges govt to postpone exams
2020-03-20
KOTTAYAM: The Nair Service Society (NSS) have urged the state government to postpone all the examinations in the state in the wake of Covid-19 scare. 

NSS general secretary G Sukumaran Nair in a press note on Thursday said that the students who have to write exams resort to unsafe ways to reach the examination centres and returning home. He also said that the students should remain in their homes at this juncture. 

The government should take emergency steps to postpone the examinations considering the seriousness of the situation, he said in the statement.

Meanwhile, MG University has announced that it will not postpone its exams. Its vice-chancellor Sabu Thomas said that the exams will be held as per schedule.
