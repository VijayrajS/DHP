Coronavirus: BJP's Bagga wants breath analyser test to be suspended
2020-03-05
NEW DELHI: Delhi BJP functionary Tajinder Pal Singh Bagga on Wednesday wrote to Delhi Police commissioner urging him to suspend breath-analyser test conducted by them police to check drunk driving. 

"In the light of the corona infection, to prevent its further spread, I request you to suspend the breath analysis test," the letter said.

The number of corona infections is soaring in the country and one case has been confirmed in Delhi. Delhi government has already scaled up efforts to tackle the situation. Breath is one of the main source to spread the virus. "Once the situation normalises, the police may resume," Bagga suggested.
