Techies set up online covid clinic
2020-03-31
Hyderabad: Software professionals industry body Telangana Information Technology Association (TITA) has decided to launch Digithon Covid-19 Online Clinic. Through this platform, it will offer medical assistance to people.

TITA founder and global president Sundeep Kumar Makthala, said, “We have seen how Indians stranded in Italy and France are struggling to get medical attention. We started this platform that allows them to get in touch with doctors .” The initiative, launched on Sunday is free, and doctors are contributing extra time towards the effort. Among those who were helped were Akhil, a student stuck in Italy. TITA also reached out to people in rural areas such as Nandimalla Cross village in Makthal. TNN
