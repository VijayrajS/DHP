Fake social media post on coronavirus triggers panic
2020-03-16
Mussoorie: A social media post stating that a person has been diagnosed with novel coronavirus in the Landour Community Hospital triggered panic among residents on Sunday. The post was uploaded by a student on Saturday late evening. In no time, the post went viral and alarmed many residents in the town. 

However, officials of Landour Community Hospital soon clarified that the post is completely fake and none of their patients has been tested coronavirus-positive.
