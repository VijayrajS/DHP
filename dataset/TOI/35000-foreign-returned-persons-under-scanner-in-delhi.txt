Covid-19 scare: 35,000 foreign returned persons under scanner in Delhi
2020-03-23
NEW DELHI: Delhi government has asked all the DMs to undertake a thorough verification exercise of around 35,000 foreign returned persons who have been staying in Delhi since March 1, 2020. 


Coronavirus in India live updates

In an order issued on Sunday, the health and family welfare department said "in the view of the global outbreak of Covid-19, it has come to the notice of the government that approximately 35,000 foreign returned persons have been staying in Delhi." Many of them have travel history to Covid-19 hotspots in different parts of the world. 

The DMs have been asked to ensure that all such persons remain in home quarantine for a minimum period of 14 days besides their contacts. Any person found disobeying the order shall be punished with fine or imprisonment under suitable laws, the order stated.
