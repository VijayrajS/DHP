Novel coronavirus threat: Three test negative in Gujarat
2020-02-14
AHMEDABAD: On Thursday, the specially set up lab for detection of novel Coronavirus (NCoV) at Civil Hospital analyzed three samples from Ahmedabad, Kheda and Vadodara. All three samples were negative, mentioned state health department officials. In total, 23 samples have been tested by National Institute of Virology (NIV), Pune and at Civil Hospital and all have been termed negative.
