Gujarat: 3 from Kadi test negative for coronavirus
2020-02-07
AHMEDABAD: Three youths from Kadi in Mehsana district, who had just returned from Thailand, were sent to the Asarwa Civil Hospital on Thursday for medical check-ups. The youths had complained of cold, sore throats and mild body pain.

Dr Gunvant Rathod, medical superintendent, Civil Hospital, said that the youths had common flu and their screening did not reveal signs associated with coronavirus. “They were provided primary treatment and were prescribed medication,” he said.

State health department officials said that the report of a 28-year-old woman from Ambli, who is quarantined at Civil Hospital for possible coronavirus infection, is expected on Friday.
