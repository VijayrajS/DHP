Coronavirus scare in Pune: Anna Hazare’s village bans entry of tourists
2020-03-14
PUNE: Residents of Ralegan Siddhi — the village of social crusader Anna Hazare — on Friday announced a ban on entry of tourists amid Covid-19 scare.


A statement issued by Hazare’s office said coronavirus seemed to be spreading in the country and prevention was the most effective option besides limiting human to human contact. “We request people not to visit the village till the threat of Covid-19 subsides,” the statement read. 

It said tourists from within the country and abroad regularly visit Ralegan Siddhi to see the watershed development work of Hazare and to meet him. Till date, over 16 lakh tourists have visited Ralegan Siddhi. TNN
