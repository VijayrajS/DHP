Mohali gets 3 more coronavirus cases
2020-03-22
MOHALI: Three more people tested positive for Covid-19 in Mohali on Saturday, taking the total number of cases to four.



Coronavirus outbreak live updates


One of them is a 74-yearold woman, the sister of the 69-year-old woman who had tested positive on Friday after she had returned from the UK, said deputy commissioner Girish Dayalan. She is in an isolation ward at a private hospital. The second case is that of a 28-year-old woman, an employee of an automobile agency. She had come in contact with the first positive case of Chandigarh. She is admitted to civil hospital, Phase VI, Mohali. The third is a 42-year-old resident. He is admitted to Government Multi-specialty Hospital, Sector 16, Chandigarh. He had returned from UK on March 12, said Dayalan.

 
 
In all, there are now 10 positive cases from the Tricity — five from Chandigarh, four from Mohali and one from Panchkula.

Dayalan said the administration has traced and quarantined 120 people who had come in contact with the four novel coronavirus casesi.

“Teams are getting addresses from the four patients and are quarantining them in their houses. Till now, 120 people have been traced and home quarantined in Mohali. In all, over 250 people have been home quarantined in various parts of the district till now,” he said.
