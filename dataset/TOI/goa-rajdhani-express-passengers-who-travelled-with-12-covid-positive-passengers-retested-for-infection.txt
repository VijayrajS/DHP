Goa: Rajdhani Express passengers who travelled with 12 Covid-positive passengers retested for infection
2020-05-22
PANAJI: Doctors from south Goa district hospital on Thursday retested 150-odd passengers who came into contact with 12 positive passengers while travelling on a Rajdhani Express train to Goa on Sunday.


These 150 passengers were contact-traced and retested as they traveled in the same compartment as the 12, who tested positive on the Sunday. The train ferried around 350 passengers.
