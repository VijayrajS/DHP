Covid-19 positive patient dies in Tamil Nadu, country's virus toll rises to 11
2020-03-25
MADURAI (TAMIL NADU): Tamil Nadu health minister C Vijayabaskar on Wednesday said that a COVID-19 positive patient passed away at a hospital here.


"Update: Despite our best efforts, the Covid-19 +ve Pt at MDU, #RajajiHospital, passed away few minutes back. He had a medical history of prolonged illness with steroid dependent COPD, uncontrolled Diabetes with Hypertension.@MoHFW_INDIA @CMOTamilNadu," Vijayabaskar tweeted.
