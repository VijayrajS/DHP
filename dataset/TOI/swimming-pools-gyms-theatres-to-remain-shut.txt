Swimming pools, gyms, theatres to remain shut amid coronavirus scare
2020-03-16
The state principal secretary, on Sunday, had issued a communication to the district collectors, civic body chiefs, commissioners and superintendents of police across the state asking them to keep swimming pools, theatres, gyms, auditoriums and museums shut in their respective jurisdictions till March 31.
