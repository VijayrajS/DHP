‘No Indian student affected by virus’
2020-01-29
Vadodara: External affairs minister S Jaishankar on Tuesday said the central government is planning to send a plane to coronavirus-affected Wuhan in China to evacuate Indians stuck there. 

Jaishankar also said that no student from India has been found to be affected by the virus till date. 

“Indian Embassy is in constant touch with the Chinese government. To evacuate Indians we are planning to send a plane to Wuhan. Our efforts are on to bring our people back. It will take some days. I urge people to trust the government on this,” Jaishankar told media on his arrival at Harni airport in the city. 

“No Indian student has been found to be affected by this virus. Parents need not worry about their children.” said Jaishankar, who is a Rajya Sabha MP from Gujarat. 

Jaishankar was on his way to Kevadiya and was received by officials and elected representatives at the airport. 

Relatives of several persons stranded in China have ben demanding that the Union government ensures that they return at the earliest.
