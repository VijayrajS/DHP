Covid-19: 12 patients discharged in Mandya
2020-06-06
MANDYA: A total 12 Covid-19 patients, including a police personnel, were discharged from MIMS on Friday, after they were cured.

The district administration held a simple ceremony to see off the patients. Senior officials including Mandya deputy commissioner M V Venkatesh and southern IGP Vipul Kumar were present. 

Meanwhile, 13 new Covid-19 cases were reported in Mandya district on Friday. All the patients had returned from Mumbai. They have been admitted in the isolation ward of the Covid hospital.
