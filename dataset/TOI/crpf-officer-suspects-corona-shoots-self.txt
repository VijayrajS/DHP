CRPF officer from Jaisalmer suspects coronavirus, shoots self
2020-05-13
JAISALMER: A 56-year-old CRPF sub-inspector from Jaisalmer shot himself with his service rifle in Jammu and Kashmir’s Anantnag district fearing that he might have been infected with Covid-19. 

Sub-inspector Fatah Singh was posted in the Mattan area of the Anantnag district.

He was rushed to the hospital where doctors declared him brought dead, CRPF sources said. He left behind a suicide note saying that, “I am afraid I may have corona. No one should touch my body. I am afraid I have corona”. 

A CRPF spokesman said, “There is no evidence that he had corona infection. We are now waiting for the test report”.
