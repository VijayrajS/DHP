Rajkot registers 1st Covid-19 death
2020-04-30
Rajkot registered its first Covid-19 casualty on Wednesday. According to health officials, Mominben Chopda, 60, a resident of Jangleshwar area succumbed to the virus on Wednesday. She was admitted to the hospital on April 20, where she developed high blood pressure during treatment. Another male patient, 47-year-old Saiyad Habibmiya Zusabmiya was discharged from the hospital on Wednesday after repeat tests proved nCov negative.
